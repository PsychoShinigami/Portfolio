1. The Great Potato War (#1 Rank)

This is arguably his most famous achievement. After a year-long,
high-intensity rivalry with im_a_squid_kid on Hypixel SkyBlock,
Techno became the #1 Potato Farmer in the world.

The Stat: Over 500 million potatoes harvested.
The Win: He didn't just out-farm his rival; he out-strategized him using maxed minions, specialized fuel, and psychological warfare

2. The $100,000 Duel vs. Dream

In 2020, MrBeast hosted a best-of-10 PvP duel
between the two biggest names in Minecraft at the time.

The Result: Technoblade won 6-4.
The Legacy: This cemented his status as one of the greatest 1.8 and 1.16 PvP players in the community.

3. The 1,400+ Bedwars Win Streak

Techno and his team (including members like Defone and Jiyn)
held a world-record win streak in Bedwars.

The Streak: 1,400 consecutive wins without a single loss.
The Ending: He famously "threw" the 1,401st game because,
he was tired of the pressure of maintaining the streak.

The Journey of a Legend

2013 - The Beginning: Alex creates the Technoblade channel, originally focusing on Studio Lore and early Minecraft content.

2017 - The Bedwars Era: Technoblade dominates Hypixel, reaching a legendary 1,400+ win streak that cemented his name in PvP history.

2019 - The Potato War: A year-long battle for the #1 Potato rank begins, resulting in over 500 million potatoes harvested.

2020 - The Great Duel: Technoblade defeats Dream in a $100,000 best-of-10 duel hosted by MrBeast, winning 6-4.

2020-2022 - The Anarchist: Joining the Dream SMP, Techno founded an anarchist commune and famously summoned withers to protect his ideals.

A Legacy of Giving

Sarcoma Foundation of America: Following his diagnosis, Technoblade and his family dedicated themselves to raising funds for cancer research.

The $500,000 Goal: During his final months, the "Technoblade Never Dies" movement raised over $500,000 for charity in record time.

Community Impact: Even now, his merchandise and continued views contribute to the Sarcoma Foundation, proving his impact goes far beyond gaming.

The Courage Award: The SFA honored Alex with the "Courage Award" for his incredible bravery and the massive support generated by his community.

Mayor of the People

The Ultimate Strategist: Technoblade didn't just play Skyblock; he solved it, using complex minion setups and specialized fuel.

The Mayoral Reign: He was famously elected as the Mayor of Skyblock, running a campaign based on "Totalitarianism" and absolute chaos.
